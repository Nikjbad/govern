The Govern Manifesto

10 pillars of governance reform that Govern demands on behalf of every Indian citizen

01
🏛️

Right to Responsive Government

Every citizen deserves a government that responds within 30 days. We demand mandatory response timelines enshrined in law.

DEMAND
02
💰

Public Fund Transparency

All government expenditure above ₹1 lakh must be publicly auditable in real-time. No secret funds, no discretionary spending without accountability.

REFORM
03
🚰

Basic Services as Fundamental Rights

Clean water, electricity, roads, and sanitation are not privileges — they are fundamental rights. Denial of these services is a punishable offence.

RIGHTS
04
⚖️

Zero Tolerance for Corruption

Corruption in civic work is theft from the public. We demand whistleblower protection, mandatory asset disclosure, and fast-track courts for civic corruption.

JUSTICE
05
📚

Equal Education for Every Child

Government schools must match private school infrastructure. Every child deserves working toilets, computers, books, and qualified teachers — non-negotiable.

EDUCATION
06
🏥

Accessible Public Healthcare

Every government hospital must have essential medicines in stock at all times. Stockouts are a criminal negligence. Citizens deserve dignity in healthcare.

HEALTH
07
🌳

Clean Environment as a Right

No illegal dumping grounds. No toxic industrial discharge near residential areas. Environmental violations must carry immediate criminal penalties.

ENVIRONMENT
08
📱

Digital Governance Now

All civic services — complaints, FIR filing, birth certificates, permits — must be accessible online within 24 hours. Digital India starts with working systems.

DIGITAL
09
👮

Police Accountability

Every police station must accept complaints without discrimination. Body cameras mandatory. Complaint tracking systems open to public audit.

ACCOUNTABILITY
10
🗳️

Community Voice in Local Governance

Ward-level budgets decided with mandatory citizen participation. No infrastructure project can be approved without a public hearing. Gram Sabha empowerment now.

DEMOCRACY
